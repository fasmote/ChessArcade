🎯 How to Play

Move the knight to visit every square on the board exactly once.

Knights move in an L-shape: 2 squares in one direction, then 1 square perpendicular.

⚡ Yellow highlighted squares show possible moves. Try to complete all squares!

🎮 Choose your board size: 4x4 (16 squares), 6x6 (36 squares), or 8x8 (64 squares)
